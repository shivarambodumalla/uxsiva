Case Study: Program Manager Efficiency Platform

The Goal

Enhance Program Managers' efficiency and bandwidth, enabling them to handle more learners with the same effort and reduce delivery operation costs.

Process

  • Research & Discovery
  • Ideation & Conceptualisation
  • Design Development
  • Feedback & Iteration
  • Finalisation & Delivery

Research & Discovery

Engaged with 20+ Program Managers, 5 BU Heads, and Senior PMs through interviews and questionnaires. Discovered pain points and insights around content import, planning, and daily ticket resolution.

  • Scattered communication & follow-up history
  • Incomplete logs of actions taken
  • Missing information impeding progress tracking
  • Lack of visibility affecting scalability
  • Difficulty in aligning cross-team/PM workflows
  • No automation for “at risk” segments

Ideation & Conceptualisation

We focused on streamlining workflows, simplifying communication, and enabling better visibility and automation across Program Managers’ tools.

Design Development

Utilised MUI (Material UI) in React to accelerate design and development using shared components between designers and developers.

Setup a reusable design library and refined flows based on real usage data and interviews.

What We Did

  • Observed PM workflows to resolve support tickets
  • Identified improvement points in their daily processes
  • Implemented automation and simplifications
  • Created a Jedi Dashboard to improve KRA tracking
  • Launched Olympus LMS enhancements

Key Solutions

  • 360° Learner Information View
  • Activity Marking & History Logs
  • Custom Views & Filters
  • Progress Tracking on KPIs (collections, reactivation, referrals)
  • Quick Filters for simplified daily operations

Discovery: Content Import

Identified inefficiencies in the weekly/daily import process. Redesigned the flow to make it faster and less error-prone.

  • Logged emails, calls, and WhatsApp interactions
  • Tracked learning & financial commitment
  • Segmented learners with one-click actions
  • Introduced follow-up reminders and sync tools

Discovery: Session Planning

Learned that daily/weekly session planning was overwhelming and error-prone. Improved interface and workflow to reduce mistakes and complexity.

Results

  • 50% increase in learner handling capacity per PM
  • Teams expanding from 100–120 to 200 learners per PM
  • Reduction in ticket resolution time
  • Enhanced visibility for planning and communication
