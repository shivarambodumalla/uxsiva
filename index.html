About

I'm a designer who specializes in UX and Interaction Design, creating digital products that are aesthetically pleasing, effortless to use, and guided by the belief that form follows function — crafting meaningful experiences that connect users and stories.

I bridge the gap between strategy and shipment. My career began in frontend engineering, giving me a deep technical understanding of how to build things that are scalable and robust. This unique blend of technical roots and empathy for users allows me to confidently lead the entire product design lifecycle.

With over 10+ years of experience, I focus on turning complex user needs into simple and functional experiences. At Great Learning, I've led key initiatives from redesigning learning systems to building internal platforms — always aiming for measurable outcomes like improved satisfaction, higher productivity, and operational savings. My toolkit includes Figma, ReactJS, and HTML/CSS, and I'm equally at home mentoring teams, building design systems, and aligning design with business strategy to create impact at scale.
