About

I'm a product designer who ships the code too. Most designers hand off specs and move on — I stay until it's in production, then I check the numbers.

Over 10+ years at Great Learning, I've designed and built AI-powered SaaS products across learning, assessment, and internal operations — from discovery through production. I owned AI products like Speed Grader, QC Checker, and a Resume Builder: defining the prompts, evaluation rules, and confidence thresholds, then building the front ends in React and Next.js with Claude Code and Cursor. That work cut manual effort by 70% and pulled validation cycles from two weeks down to three days.

Along the way I built an enterprise design system that cut UI inconsistencies by 60% and sped delivery by 35%, plus an AI design-system assistant that helps designers and engineers generate on-brand UI. Move me to strategy and I've driven ₹50Cr+ in annual impact, grown engagement 32%, and led a CRM migration off LeadSquared. I'm equally at home in a Figma file, a pull request, or a roadmap — often in the same week.

Currently looking for: a Principal or Staff Product Designer role on an AI-powered SaaS product, where designing the experience and building the front end happen together.
